CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

-You will be given a random number as your goal at the start of the game.

-There are four valuable crystals below, each worth a different ammount. However the value of each crystal is hidden from you.

-Each time you click on a crystal, the value of that crystal will be added to your total score.

-Win the game by matching your score to the goal. However if you go over the goal, you lose!
